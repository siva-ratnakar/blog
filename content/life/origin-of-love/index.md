In the long stretch of past lives, was this thirst always hidden?<br>
Only now, in the sparkle of her smile and shade of her eyes,<br>
Does a brand-new, never-known season begin to dance<br>

A silence of affection that vibrates through every atom,<br>
A soul that refuses to stay still,<br>
urged by an unknown call to rush for something<br>

This restlessness in the chest is but a sculptor's hand,<br>
Carving a soul into the very walls where your breath began.<br>
Like the heavens answering to steal this restless heart,<br>
With the melody of a blissful hymn, something new is born<br>

Can your eyes ever see the wind that carries the scent of the jasmine?<br>
Can the shore ever measure the depth of the sea’s longing?<br>
Just as the flute doesn't know the song until the breath passes through,<br>
Your life did not know this melody until the stir of something new.<br>

To a tiny sprout drowning in its own thirst,<br>
She is a raindrop that makes the petals burst,<br>
The very drop shall become the dawn of spring,<br>
Rising as the destiny you never knew your heart could bring; maybe it's love.<br>

Unless you ask the burning forge,<br>
The earth cannot prove it is a diamond and not mere clay.<br>
If your burning heart is asked what her presence means,<br>
Then the proof is the boon of love that arrives to remain.<br>

Does the river, leaping past its banks, ever know<br>
Which first raindrop fueled its wild, surging flow?<br>
Does the flower, basking in the sun's golden glow,<br>
Recall which first stone whispered for it to grow?<br>

This is a meeting of the sky and the earth,<br>
Yet no one could point and say where it actually began<br>
It is a truth that needs no witness to stand,<br>
A light that needs no lamp held in a hand<br>

Is there a date on the calendar to mark when life truly begins?<br>
A moment to witness when love dawns in a heart?<br>
If love could be defined by a simple word,<br>
Would that word truly understand its own depth?<br>

It is a story history has never learned to read,<br>
An emotion even poetry fails to sow a seed,<br>
A sweetness that knows no scales or rhymes,<br>
A sacred stillness outside the boundaries of time.<br>

The moment your glances locked,<br>
Time itself stood still to take a breath.<br>
Your heart forgets its scars and burning flame,<br>
Every instant she utters your name.<br>

Such is this pull—a gravity that doesn't bring you down,<br>
But lifts you up until the stars are within your grasp.<br>
And now, as your heart’s desire awakens in you,<br>
Tomorrow’s destiny already shines in her gaze.<br>

If there is a companion to walk beside you,<br>
Even a stumble becomes a rhythmic dance.<br>
With this person to share all your great moments,<br>
Even your demon becomes a playful joker by chance.<br>

And if you ever wonder when it all truly began,<br>
You will find no answer in time—<br>
Only in the quiet certainty<br>
That somewhere between your breath and the light in her eyes,<br>
You ceased to be two, and became the horizon where the sun must rise.<br>
